Quick answers

Three simple Artemis questions

What is Artemis trying to do?

NASA says Artemis aims to return humans to the Moon, enable scientific discovery, and prepare for future Mars missions.

What happened most recently?

According to NASA mission pages, Artemis II launched on April 1, 2026 and completed a crewed lunar flyby.

What comes after Artemis II?

NASA's Artemis III page currently lists a 2027 mission focused on integrated operations with commercial systems.
